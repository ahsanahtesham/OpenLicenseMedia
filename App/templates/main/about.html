About Open License Media Search

Our Mission

Open License Media Search is a platform dedicated to making openly licensed media content more accessible to everyone. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly interface for discovering high-quality images and audio that can be freely used, modified, and shared, while properly attributing creators.

What is Openverse?

Openverse is a search engine for openly licensed media maintained by WordPress. It indexes creative works from multiple sources that are either in the public domain or licensed under Creative Commons licenses, making them freely available for creators and users.

Our application integrates with the Openverse API to provide you access to this vast collection of openly licensed media.

Understanding Open Licenses

Open licenses, such as Creative Commons licenses, provide a standardized way for creators to grant permission to others to use their work under certain conditions. These licenses make it clear which uses are permitted, enabling creators to share their work while retaining certain rights.

When using content from our platform, always make sure to check the specific license terms and provide appropriate attribution to the creator.
